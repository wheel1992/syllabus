Meet the team

We are a team of dedication and determination to ensure our students achieve better examination results and appreciate Chinese language in the current society.

Pamela Teo

FULL-TIME PRIVATE TUTOR

In Singapore, Chinese studies have been an issue since the rising of English education. Students of mine are from different races, such as Chinese, Indians and Eurasians and they have been struggling with their Chinese as their second language, Mother Tongue. My aims and mission is to educate them the correct techniques in learning Chinese. Hence, Syllabus is created to aid their learning by understanding the Chinese words and applying it in both school and daily life.
